Book Cover

Tool(s): Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Firefly & Chat GPT

Project Description: I used Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Firefly & Chat GPT to make this book cover. I first started by asking Chat GPT to make a book cover for a book with a fantasy genre. After I found a title and a subtitle, I asked Adobe Firefly to create a book cover using that title. Once I got both the title and the cover, I used Photoshop to add the title to the generated cover.

Project caption: The Knight and the Dragon's Curse - A Knight's Courage Tested.
