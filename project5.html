Sound Design

Tool(s): Adobe Premiere Pro

Project Description: I used Adobe Premiere Pro to make this video. I selected a video from Canvas that had no sound. Once I gathered the sounds that I wanted to include, I put them over the clips. I trimmed the audios so they matched up with the corresponding clips and added transisitons to make the sounds change more smoothly.
