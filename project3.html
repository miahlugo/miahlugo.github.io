New Species

Tool(s): Adobe Photoshop

Project Description: I used Adobe Photoshop to create this new species. I used the body of a sea otter, the ears of a panda, and the horn of a narwhal. I used the masking and blending tools to blend parts of the different animals together.

Project caption: A Narwhal, Sea Otter, and a Panda.
